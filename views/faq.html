Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is this thing?

Its a cartoon strip generator, allowing you to make cartoon strips with all your favourite characters from Digitiser, like Mr T, Insincere Dave, and Lewis and Morse.

2. Digitiser? What's that?

Digitiser was a daily computer game magazine running on Channel 4's Teletext service from January 1993 to March 2003. Written by Mr. Biffo on his own for most of its existence, it was the funniest thing in print for a long period of time. More information on Digitiser can be found at these excellent sites:
Moleman's Digitiser Archive, Super Page 58, and Mr Biffo's Digitiser Obituary Section.

3. And who's Mr Biffo?

Mr. Biffo, also known as Paul Rose, is a writer for TV shows these days. Look here for a more detailed explanation of his life and times.

4. How does this cartoon strip generator work, then?

Choose the characters that you want from the selection boxes, and type the text you want them to say in the text boxes. And then press 'Create'. Its as easy as that.

5. There's so many characters to choose - how do I know which is which?

The characters page contains a list of all the characters, with their pictures and their names, too.

6. Can I save the cartoons I've made?

Technically, no. Although if you save the address of your cartoon strip, you can revisit the strip whenever you like, or send it to someone you hate.
